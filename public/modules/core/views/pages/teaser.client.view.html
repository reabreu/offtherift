Our builder has the advantage of providing real-time champion statistics as you set up your own builds.

We chose to open it as a Beta so you can help shape the final version with us.

Right now, the stats are calculated based on items, runes, masteries and champion's growth and base statistics

We do plan on adding the champion abilites as the final piece of the builder and making some other cool tools for you guys.

Creating your own build

1

The app will fetch the base stats and show them to you.
2

As you do the app will recalculate the stats.
